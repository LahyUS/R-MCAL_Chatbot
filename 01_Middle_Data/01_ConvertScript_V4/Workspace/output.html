Should-have knowledge for a better qualified code

•     By understanding the basic concept of AUTOSAR ADC, one may be able to implement it. Still these knowledge must be know if one aim for the perfection

•     The conversion time of ADC hardware is vary and may be between devices, knowledge of the maximum and minimum required time for conversion can be quite useful.

•     Accuracy of ADC hardware is vary, knowledge of such values (tolerance at one specific converted data) will always be useful in specific cases.

•     ADC contains various safety mechanisms to ensure the correctness of it, knowing how it works and what may cause the malfunction of such function are always of use.
